Liopleurodon - Smooth-sided Teeth
"lie-oh-ploor-oh-don"

Length: 7m
Height: 3m
Area: Europe
Climate: Tropical
Period: Jurassic
Family: Pliosauridae

Liopleurodons were piscivores and carnivores, 
feeding on giant fish, sharks, Ichthyosaurs, 
and even other pliosaurs.

Liopleurodon's size is a controversial topic 
since BBC displayed the creature at 25m when in
reality the average is that of 7 meters.
