Tyrannosaurus
"ty-ran-o-sawr-us"

Length: 12-13m
Height: 4-4.3m
Area: North America
Climate: Sub-Tropical
Period: Cretaceous
Family: Tyrannosauridae

Tyrannosaurus were carnivores, feeding 
on hadrosaurid and ceratopsian dinosaurs.

It is famous for its role in the movie 
Jurassic Park. Its bones were hollow, 
allowing for swift movement.
